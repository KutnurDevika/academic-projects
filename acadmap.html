About AcadMap

AcadMap is a Smart Academic Roadmap Planning Dashboard System designed to help students organize, track, and optimize their academic journey. Many students struggle with unstructured planning and unclear career goals. Our system provides a guided framework for academic success.

By integrating Data Structures and Algorithms concepts such as Linked Lists, Stacks, and Queues, the platform ensures efficient management of goals, tasks, and academic progress.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide students with a structured academic planning system that improves clarity, productivity, and performance.

🧠

Technical Foundation

Built using HTML, CSS, Java, and core DSA concepts to ensure efficient academic tracking and roadmap generation.

🚀

Future Vision

To evolve into a full academic intelligence system with AI-driven insights and personalized career recommendations.
